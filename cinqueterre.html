Cinque Terre

To capture the essence of Cinque Terre, I began by researching its history, culture, and art to inspire my sketches. After gathering a substantial amount of information, I moved to my sketchpad and developed several initial ideas. Through my research, I discovered that anchovies are a staple ingredient in many dishes from Cinque Terre, often referred to as the "bread of the sea" due to their abundance and importance in the local cuisine.

In my research, I also discovered that Cinque Terre is made up of five villagesâ€” Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore. Each village has its own unique charm, traditions, and strong connection to the sea, which plays a central role in the region's culture and daily life. This is why I chose to incorporate five fish into my logo design to symbolically represent the five villages and highlight the importance of the sea to the identity and livelihood of Cinque Terre.
