Hello!

I'm Angelica a graphic designer based in Honolulu, Hawaii. I am passionate about my crafts with a solid foundation in design fundamentals, combining creativity, attention to detail, and dedication in every project. I focus on delivering visually impactful designs that achieve the project objective. I'm committed to continuous growth through hands-on experience and collaboration, while working closely with teams to ensure clear communication and a smooth process from start to finish.

Outside of design, I enjoy a slower pace of life, often unwinding by baking or getting lost in a good book. I also find peace and inspiration by sitting at the beach, enjoying the sunset, and reflecting on the world around me. These moments of tranquility allow me to recharge and bring fresh ideas into my professional work.
