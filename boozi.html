Boozi Hard Seltzer

For this project, I was tasked with creating a typographic hierarchy on a retail product. I had the freedom to choose any drink brand, and I decided to create a hard seltzer. Once I settled on that direction, I began thinking about how to design the layout to resonate with my target audience—adults aged 21 and over.

I wanted to connect the flavor to a tropical, refreshing vibe and landed on passion fruit, inspired by Hawaii. To create the image of the passion fruit, I used a bit of AI-generated imagery and refined it in Photoshop to get the exact look I was aiming for.

For the logo, I experimented with a mix of analog and digital techniques. I printed and scanned the letter “B,” moving it side to side during the scan to create a ripple effect that mimics the motion of waves—tying back to the tropical, beachy theme of the brand.
